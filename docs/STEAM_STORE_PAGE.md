## Full Description

**TAKE COMMAND OF STRATEGIC DEFENSE**

NORAD War Simulator puts you in the commander's seat of the North American Aerospace Defense Command. Monitor global threats via satellite early warning systems, coordinate interceptor launches across multiple defense layers, and make split-second decisions to protect millions of lives.

**KEY FEATURES**

🌍 **Globe-Based Simulation**
- Realistic Earth model with accurate geography
- Track missile threats across continents
- Satellite early warning network (DSP, SBIRS, GPS-III)

🚀 **Realistic Ballistic Physics**
- True-to-life missile trajectories
- Boost, midcourse, and terminal phases
- Multiple interceptor types (GBI, THAAD, Patriot)

🎯 **Multiple Game Modes**
- **Scenarios**: 6 built-in scenarios from tutorials to global war
- **Campaign**: 8-mission campaign with tech tree progression
- **Scenario Editor**: Create and share custom scenarios
- **Multiplayer**: Co-op and Versus modes

⚙️ **Technology Tree**
- Research upgrades between missions
- Enhance interceptor effectiveness
- Improve satellite detection range

🌐 **Steam Integration**
- 18 achievements
- Cloud saves
- Workshop support for custom scenarios
- Leaderboards

**GAMEPLAY**

As NORAD commander, you'll:

1. **Monitor**: Use satellites to detect missile launches worldwide
2. **Track**: Follow threats through boost, midcourse, and terminal phases
3. **Decide**: Choose which interceptors to deploy and when
4. **Defend**: Protect cities using layered defense systems

**REALISM**

NORAD War Simulator is built on realistic physics and military doctrine:

- Great circle missile trajectories
- Shoot-look-shoot doctrine
- Layered defense architecture
- Accurate city locations and populations

**MULTIPLAYER**

Team up with friends in Co-op mode to defend against escalating threats, or compete in Versus mode where one team launches while the other defends.

**MODDING**

Create custom scenarios with the built-in editor and share them via Steam Workshop.
